About.

OFF is an advocacy group founded in 2026 at the University of Michigan. We are an independent initiative demanding transparency and accountability from American universities in the role they have played in disrupting their own students' workforce outcomes. We exist to support the economic rights of all young professionals and working class citizens in the face of AI-driven disruption.

More broadly, we believe in equal access to AI technologies. These systems were trained on humanity's collective knowledge, creativity, and labor. In many cases, they draw from vast bodies of copyrighted work. As such, private corporations have no rightful claim to the AI systems they have created. The benefits and dangers of this technology are immense, meaning the risks must be navigated collectively, and the proceeds shared equally. Workers, students, and creators all deserve a stake in the systems they have made possible.

The OFF Team can be reached via email at info@offmovement.org
