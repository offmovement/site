AI EXPOSURE RUBRIC
Each school is rated by AI exposure level, a cumulative measure of AI investments, partnerships, industry funding, and more. The vast majority of ratings are labelled as incomplete due to endowment nondisclosure policies. Nevertheless, we ranked them with the information that was available. Note: Many schools fund AI initiatives aimed at public-good applications, such as biomedical research, climate solutions, and sustainable agriculture. These projects are not counted in the rating, and related information may be absent from a school's description.

Symbol Level of AI Exposure What It Means
Extreme AI is structurally redefining this institution. This category includes investments, gifts, and military contracts worth hundreds of millions, or confirmed endowment stakes in AI companies.
Confirmed Significant AI exposure that falls short of institution-defining. This category includes sub-$200M AI gifts or programs, formal industry co-research structures where corporations participate in academic research, major partnerships with AI corporations, or AI endowment exposure.
Moderate These institutions may have smaller scale or internal AI programs. They are not a defining or standout feature of the institution.
Low These institutions have negligible AI partnerships or programs. They fully disclose their endowment holdings at a high or itemized level. They are hardly entangled with the AI industry.
Undisclosed No public AI investments listed and no significant identified partnerships on record. Exposure cannot be confirmed or ruled out. The absence of evidence is not evidence of absence.
