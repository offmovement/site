Students trade tuition, labor, and years of their lives to secure their future. Higher education institutions aggressively market job placement rates and return on investment to justify tuition hikes. Students borrow against those metrics.

Meanwhile, institutional endowments are actively funding the companies and infrastructure built to make our degrees worthless. Then collecting the profits when it succeeds.

The disruptive impact of artificial intelligence has been obvious for years. To take our money, destroy our careers, and profit from both sides is not a technicality. It constitutes fraud.

Now, graduates are left to face a collapsing job market.

Demand restitution. Make them make it right.

Our demands
  • 01
    Restitution.
    Universities bet against the very future they sold us. AI-related endowment assets and profits must be transferred to an independently administered Student Restitution Trust to address our economic displacement.
  • 02
    Full Disclosure.
    Universities must provide complete transparency on all endowment holdings tied to workplace automation and generative AI. This includes private equity, venture capital, and direct stakes.
  • 03
    Decoupling.
    We demand immediate severance of all corporate partnerships, research sponsorships, and recruitment arrangements with firms whose core business model is the algorithmic replacement of human workers.
