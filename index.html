Turn Your Phone Into a Dumbphone

1. Delete or disable all social media apps.

2. Disable Safari + App Store.

3. Put your Screen Time password in a notebook & hide it.

4. Move your phone out of reach: drawer, bag, other room.

5. Use "Beeper" to receive DMs without algorithmic feeds.

6. Keep only calls, texts, maps, & essential tools.

7. Join an OFF group for accountability + real community.

About OFF

Have a skill you’ve been meaning to learn? A book you just can’t bring yourself to finish? Tired of endless scrolling eating up your time? Then OFF is for you.

Ø symbolizes *empty space* — the quiet your brain almost never gets anymore. OFF is a student-led movement to rebuild our attention spans, our friendships, and our future. It’s about creating a culture where being present, connecting deeply, and reclaiming our time isn’t unusual — where living in the real world instead of through screens is the norm.

The Circles

Twice a month, join a tight-knit meetup to check in on your goals, trade tips for unplugging, and hang out with people who get it.

Events

In-person card nights, board games, movie screenings, study tables, walks. People keep saying the same thing: “It feels like the old days.”

The Mission

To escape algorithmic control, rebuild attention spans, restore real-world connection, and reclaim our lives — starting with college campuses.
